-Welcome to Zazzy-

In an age where music is virtually free and everything else is little more than a click away on the Internet, the youth have turned their attentions (and their wallets) to fashion and clothes as a means of expressing themselves. In any major developed city around the world, you’re likely to see kids wearing box logos so for example the classic Supreme white on red box logo t shirt, as a means of showing off their wealth and individuality. Style-conscious consumers, from your 6-year-old cousin to your law firm partner uncle, are looking for a fresh pair of Yeezys, and Palace Skateboards is globally-reowned as being “better for bunning”.

In this website, you’ll be able to learn about 4 of the best high-end and street brands along with a shout out to some personal favourite brands. The brands will be judged on history, inspiration, availability, and the hype around that particular brand.
